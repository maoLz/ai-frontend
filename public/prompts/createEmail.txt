{
  "subject": "Security Advisory: Multiple Failed Login Attempts at __COMPANY_NAME__",
  "locale": "en",
  "requiredVars": [
    "Head_Banner_Url",
    "USER_NAME",
    "COMPANY_NAME",
    "Device_Name",
    "Device_ID",
    "Date",
    "Time",
    "Rebell_Merchant_Url"
  ],
  "description": "Security advisory email for failed PIN login attempts"
}

subject 从上下文中获取
requiredVars需要与email中内容名称对应，前后不能有__符号
subject中如果包含变量名,前后需要为__符号
description 根据邮件内容自动补齐
