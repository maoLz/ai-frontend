1.1 模版注意事项:
保留模版样式
html中变量名称请使用__变量名__

2.emailName.schema.json

json参考模版:
{
  "subject": "Notice: Your Invitation to Join __Entity_Name__ on Rebell Has Been Withdrawn",
  "locale": "en",
  "requiredVars": [
    "Entity_Name",
    "Recipient_Role",
    "Head_Banner_Url",
    "Rebell_Merchant_Url"
  ],
  "description": "Notification email informing the recipient that their invitation to join a Rebell merchant account has been withdrawn."
}

2.1模版注意事项:
subject 从上下文中获取
requiredVars需要与email中内容名称对应，前后不能有__符号
subject中如果包含变量名,前后需要为__符号
description 根据邮件内容自动补齐
